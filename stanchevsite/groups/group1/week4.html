Project Planning Step By Step

Step 1: Project Goals

The first step of defining the goals is to identify the list of stakeholders in the project. The following list of people are the stakeholders in the project:

  • Devin Holland
  • Josh Lindoo
  • Tim Starr
  • Professor Stanchev
  • Kettering University Students
  • FIRST Robotics high school students

Each of us are on the list because we are the ones who are making and also will be using the final product. We are the ones who are sponsoring the project since it is done on our own without pay. Professor Stanchev is on the list because he is the “Sponsor” of the project and we are doing it under his guidance.

Now that we have the list of stakeholders we have to list out the needs of the users. The list of needs for the project (in prioritized order) are as follows:

  • System for access to the FIRST robotics center
  • Proposal system for FIRST center and other aspects of Kettering University
  • Forums for FIRST center.

The forums is at the bottom of the needs list. This is because there are already forums for FIRST and this would just be specific to the center. The first and second bullets should be along the same priority levels, the proposal system is good for all of Kettering, not just the center, and the access to the FIRST center can be fleshed out and improved to handle usage of the center efficiently.

Now that we have the goals of the project laid out, we move on to look at the Project Deliverables.

Step 2: Project Deliverables

In this section we will list the deliverables needed to complete the goals listed in Step 1. The deliverables are as follows:

  • Database of Users including permissions and user information that will allow for access and record keeping in the FIRST robotics center
  • Database and front end form for the proposal system for proposals to be entered, logged and approved or denied.
  • Database and front end for forums for users.

Now that we listed the deliverables, we need to schedule out the time and resources for the deliverables.

  • Database of Users including permissions and user information that will allow for access and record keeping in the FIRST robotics center.

Now we need to lay out the Supporting Plans

Step 4: Supporting Plans

Human Resource Plan

The individuals with leading roles in the project are the 3 group members, Devin Holland, Josh Lindoo and Tim Starr. The number of people needed to carry out the project is the 3 members of our group.

The people who need to be kept informed about the project are the group members and Professor Stanchev.
