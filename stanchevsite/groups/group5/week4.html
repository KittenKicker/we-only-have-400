Group #5: WKUF Site(Devin Holland, Lucas Brandt, Jacob Miller)


Project Planning Step by Step

Step 1: Project Goals

The first step of defining the goals is to identify the list of stakeholders in the project. The following list of people are the stakeholders in the project:

  • Lucas Brandt
  • Devin Holland
  • Jacob Miller
  • Professor Stanchev
  • Kettering Radio Club WKUF
  • Flint Community members
  • Fans of the radio station

Each of us are on the list because we are the ones who are making and also will be using the final product. We are the ones who are sponsoring the project since it is done on our own without pay. Professor Stanchev is on the list because he is the “Sponsor” of the project and we are doing it under his guidance.

Now that we have the list of stakeholders we have to list out the needs of the users. The list of needs for the project (in prioritized order) are as follows:

  • On demand radio
  • Song request system
  • Website update

Website update is at the bottom of the list because the existing features of the website are already functioning correctly and are just in need of a visual overhaul. This would be the last step after we implement the new features. The on demand radio is a very cool feature that would draw people to the website, we should prioritize this first. Song request is a staple on many radio websites and therefore it is still a high priority on our list.

Now that we have the goals of the project laid out, we move on to look at the Project Deliverables.

Step 2: Project Deliverables

In this section we will list the deliverables needed to complete the goals listed in Step 1. The deliverables are as follows:

  • A web based MP3 player that will allow the users to search for and queue up DJ broadcasts that are uploaded to the website.
  • A form page on the website that will allow users to input their song requests
  • Updated existing pages on the website

Now that we listed the deliverables, we need to schedule out the time and resources for the deliverables.

Step 3: Project Schedule

The time & resource allocations for the Project Deliverables are as follows:

  • On demand radio
  • This is a big feature with many parts.
  • Time: 50%
  • Resources: 2 group members
  • Song request system
  • Time: 35%
  • Resources: 1 group member
  • Updated existing website pages
  • Time: 15%
  • Resources: 3 group members after first 2 deliverables are finished

Now we need to lay out the Supporting Plans.

Step 4: Supporting Plans

Human Resource Plan

The individuals with leading roles in the project are the 3 group members, Devin Holland, Lucas Brandt and Jacob Miller. The number of people needed to carry out the project is the 3 members of our group.

Communications Plan

The people who need to be kept informed about the project are the group members and Professor Stanchev. Since this system can be implemented as a standalone and then incorporated into WKUF’s website, the communication with them will be minimal. We will need to keep them updated with the processes they will need to follow to add our features to their system.

Risk Management Plan

Since this is a class project and is realistic for the class time, there is not much risk involved in attempting the project.
