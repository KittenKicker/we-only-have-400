Project Goals

Create database for storage of all members of clubs including the Greek Community. Also, a

way to find out when clubs are holding meetings and if they are open to visitors. This would improve

club participation and advertisement. It would also allow people who do not attend the Carnival of

Clubs to find out when clubs are meeting and try them out. You could also do a sort on who the

presidents of all the clubs or Greek houses are and include a feature to email them all instantly. This

would help improve communication.
